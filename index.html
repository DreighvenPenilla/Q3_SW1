WANDAVISION

SYNOPSIS

Wanda Maximoff and her family live happily in a world painted like a sitcom. However, their life is constantly interrupted by the interference of entities outside of their world. Could these entities be a dangerous outerwordly threat? Or are these entities from the same world they live in?

CHARACTERS

Wanda Maximoff

Vision
Wanda Maximoff (played by Elizabeth Olsen) is a loving wife to her husband, Vision. She is not your ordinary housewife because she has some magic tricks up her sleeves!
Vision (played by Paul Bethany) is your typical caring and hardworking husband. However, he is not your typical "human" husband. Behind the scenes, gears roll and his disguises fall.

Agnes
Agnes (played by Kathryn Hahn) is one of those nosy neighbors meddling in others' business. She's a fun woman to be around, however, as she can lighten up the atmosphere with her hilarious humor. She's not so suspicious, is she?
